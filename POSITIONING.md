# UNISIN — Positioning

## Core Positioning
Unisin is a unified inbox for creators to manage brand and professional messages across email and social platforms — without missing opportunities.

## What Matters
Unisin prioritizes messages tied to monetization, collaboration, and professional outreach. The product is intentionally focused on signal over volume.

## Initial Supported Platforms (MVP)
- Email (Gmail)
- Instagram Business
- LinkedIn Pages

## Deferred Platforms
- Discord
- X (Twitter)
- Reddit
- YouTube

## Non-Goals (Initial)
- Personal inbox aggregation
- AI auto-replies
- Content scheduling
- Outbound CRM features
- Mobile-first apps
